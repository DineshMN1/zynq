# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
